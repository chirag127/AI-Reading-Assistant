Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Reading Assistant ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data.

By installing and using AI Reading Assistant, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Required Permissions

AI Reading Assistant requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the currently active tab. This is necessary to access the content of the webpage you're viewing for summarization, key point extraction, and term explanation.
  • storage: Enables the extension to save your preferences and settings (such as summary mode, speech rate, and voice selection) locally on your device.
  • scripting: Allows the extension to execute scripts on the pages you visit to provide features like text-to-speech and highlighting.
  • host permissions (https://ai-reading-assistant.onrender.com/*): Allows the extension to communicate with our backend server to process AI requests for summarization, key point extraction, and term explanations.

Information We Collect

AI Reading Assistant collects the following information:

  • Content from active webpages: When you use the extension's features (summarize, extract key points, or explain terms), the content from the active webpage or your selected text is temporarily processed.
  • User preferences: Your settings such as summary mode, speech rate, pitch, voice selection, and toolbar state are stored locally on your device.

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • Login credentials
  • Financial information
  • Any data from websites you visit but don't use our extension on

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the extension (summarization, key point extraction, term explanation, and text-to-speech)
  • To save and apply your preferences and settings
  • To improve the extension's performance and features

We do not sell, rent, or share any of your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

When you use the AI features (summarize, extract key points, explain), the content is temporarily sent to our secure backend server, which communicates with Google's Gemini API to process your request. This content is not stored permanently and is only used to generate the requested AI response.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

Third-Party Services

AI Reading Assistant uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: Used to process AI requests for summarization, key point extraction, and term explanations. Your content is processed according to Google's AI Privacy Policy.
  • Web Speech API: A browser-native API used for text-to-speech functionality. This processing happens locally in your browser and does not send data to external servers.

User Rights

As a user of AI Reading Assistant, you have the right to:

  • Access and modify your preferences and settings at any time through the extension's popup
  • Uninstall the extension, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Request information about what data is being processed

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
